RFS-3 gets hit by Asteroids

The asteroid continued to break apart as it approached RFS-3. RFS-3 was hit multiple times by asteroids. 

When Sally saw that it was inevitable that the asteroid was going to hit RFS-3, she immediately placed a distress call asking for anyone in the vicinity, to head directly towards the refueling station, and be ready to execute a rescue mission. A short time later the asteroid hunk slammed into RFS-3. Half of the station was destroyed on impact. Three fourths of the crew were lost on impact. Meanwhile, beneath the dock, a hidden mechanism would engage, activating a series of synchronized movements that would unlock a concealed portal connecting the dock to the vault's interior. Under the cover of darkness and the distraction of refueling operations, the precious metals would be discreetly unloaded from the mining ships and transferred into the vault's secure chambers.

To further enhance the disguise, Sally devised a system of automated sensors and monitoring devices that would detect any anomalies or unauthorized access to the vault. These sensors would be seamlessly integrated into the station's existing security infrastructure, ensuring that the vault's true purpose remained undetected.

With Sally's meticulous planning and execution, the storage vault became an invisible fortress, a hidden treasure trove, concealed within the heart of RFS-3. The precious metals from the cosmos would reside in this clandestine sanctuary, their existence known only to a select few, a testament to Sally's ingenuity and strategic brilliance.
