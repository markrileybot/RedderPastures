Epilogue - 2065

Mars 2065. Where are they now? Ten years later "the band" had a reunion. Cody and Dr. Dumas are living comfortably in the ZON. Scotty Armstrong is still being Scotty, he has made, and lost three fortunes over the last ten years. Captain Russell is still flying but he is now based on the Moon. Betty is now an executive producer at GNN. The Earth is in worse shape and Big Daddy Phil has many more stories to tell.
