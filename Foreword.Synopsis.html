Foreword - 2050

Big Daddy Phil, a legendary singer-songwriter who started his folk career in his seventies, recounts the tales of his great-granddaughter Betty Ballena. Betty is an investigative reporter for GNN.  She became quite famous, very successful, and was quite the figure on the world stages.  She operated on Earth at first, but then moved on to the Moon and eventually to Mars. Big Daddy Phil recounts Betty and her associates adventures.
